Lembas Bread

Description

Inspired by the Lord of the Rings trilogy, this Lembas Bread recipe is sweet and filling! Prep and cook time is approximately 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2/3 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon honey

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees Farenheit.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk flour, baking powder and salt together.
  3. Add butter now and mix until fine granules form, then add sugar and cinnamon. Mix them thoroughly.
  4. Finally add the cream, honey and vanilla. Stir them until nice thick dough forms.
  5. Roll the dough out about 1/2 inch thickness evenly and cut out 3 inch squares onto a cookie sheet.
  6. Bake for about 12 minutes or more (depending on thickness) until lightly golden.
  7. Serve after cooled.
